About The Book

The Fulfilled

On August 28, 2023

If fulfillment was a person,she would be that child that makes every father smile, or perhaps your favorite meal when cooked just the way you like, or that instrumentalist that knows just the perfect sound to give. It is the reason why we wake up to each day, the reason why we plan and make effort.

The Fulfilled...

It is the reason why we must fulfill purpose, and at such; we in this world of uncertainties, doubts, failures, unforeseen circumstances and an unending path to knowledge must get the understanding of life, purpose, success and uniqueness so we can gain that feeling of fulfillment and peace at the end of the voyage.

The Fulfilled...

As individuals we are caught up in a dissected life cutting across two worlds; the material and the spiritual, while life its self appears overwhelming to comprehend. We are faced with a reality that may just be a puzzle or a game without a perfect form; but alas! we are saved by facts, principles, experiences, and teaching to help us navigate through its rage.

The Fulfilled...

Coming to understand how reckless we can be without proper knowledge, this book has put some of my knowledge around fulfillment to help the readers gain fulfillment in any possible way I can. I wish that everyone reading this book is a young, as the main purpose is to shape our mindset around fulfillment and also eliminate the possible barriers of living unfulfilled.
